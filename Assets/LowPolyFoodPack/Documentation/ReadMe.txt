------------------------------------------
📄 Description:
------------------------------------------
This asset is perfect for mobile games, casual titles, or stylized 3D environments, and includes a variety of stylized low poly food models of such category's:
Bread & Bakery,
Dairy & Eggs,
Drinks,
Fast Food,
Fruits,
Meat & Seafood,
Miscellaneous food,
Snacks & Sweets,
Vegetables.

Each model is set up as a prefab with correct pivot placement and materials already assigned.

📌 How to Use:
------------------------------------------
1. Drag the prefab from the `Prefabs/` folder into your scene.
2. All prefabs come with the correct materials and pivot points.
3. You may customize materials or textures as needed.
